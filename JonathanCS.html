Tigres UNAL History:

Tigres was founded late and still managed to become one of the biggest teams in mexico.

Tigres was the first mexican team to play in La Copa Libertadors (cup in brazil).

They managed to get to the finals but go elimated by River Plate (Argentina Club) with a score

of 3-0. They have a total of 7 league trophies and 7 international trophies.

Through out the year of 2010-2020 Tigres Unal was named Team of the Decade in the Mexican League
